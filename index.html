Research Participant Information

We define musical predisposition as:

  • You play a musical instrument on a regular basis currently, or
  • You have played a musical instrument on a regular basis for more than a year in the last 5 years, or
  • You have received formal musical education in the last 5 years, or
  • You consider yourself musically-inclined for reasons not listed above.

Instructions

On the following screen, select the play button to begin listening to a melody. As the melody plays, the progress indicator will identify the current measure by highlighting it. During or after listening, guess which chord is associated with each measure by dragging and dropping chords into each block.
